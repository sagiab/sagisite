Web 3.0 as i see it

I see it's as a combination between an effective ways of connecting and integrating data and information from various data sets to one stream , and Artificial intelligence(natural language processing, machine learning, machine reasoning, autonomous agents). all of that for making the web 3.0 browser to act like a personal assistant. in a simple word , in web 3.0 all of the efforts and technologies focusing on that machine's understanding of information in ordered to provide us (the humans) a better service(more productive and intuitive user experience).

Web 3.0 sites

      Amazon.com - Amazon has a "recommender system," which tracks what you've looked at and bought, and uses that information to recommend new products to you. In addition Amazon has a massive database(servers) and cloud.
      netflix.com - netflix like amazon has a recommend system that "know" the use , Netflix has over 69 million members in over 60 countries enjoying more than 100 million hours of TV shows whats gives them a tremendous power on the web(get alot of information and learn the users).
